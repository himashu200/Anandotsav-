About Anandotsav Exhibition

Anandotsav Exhibition is a premier exhibition organizer, powered by SAVANT CREATION. Based in Sawantwadi, Maharashtra, we have curated numerous successful exhibitions in various cities across India. Our mission is to bridge the gap between creators, entrepreneurs, and the audience through vibrant events.

We specialize in large-scale exhibitions, thematic showcases, wedding expos, handmade product fairs, and lifestyle events. Our dedicated team ensures flawless execution — from venue design to vendor coordination. With each exhibition, we aim to spread joy and opportunities, making every event an “Anandotsav” (celebration of bliss).
